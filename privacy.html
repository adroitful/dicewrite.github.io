DiceWrite Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 20, 2026

DiceWrite ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Rollsmith collects, uses, and protects information when you use our application.

Information We Collect

Rollsmith does not require you to create an account and does not collect personally identifiable information.

The app may use the following device features:

  • Microphone (for optional voice dice rolling)
  • Photos (only when you choose a custom background)
  • Local device storage (to save settings and preferences)

Voice Recognition

If you enable voice commands, speech recognition is provided through your device's operating system. DiceWrite does not record, store, or transmit your voice to our own servers.

Photos

If you choose a custom background image, Rollsmith requests access to your photo library. Selected images remain on your device and are not uploaded to DiceWrite.

Local Storage

Rollsmith stores settings such as dice configurations, appearance preferences, voice settings, and other app preferences locally on your device. This information never leaves your device.

Advertising

The free version of Rollsmith displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain information as described in Google's own Privacy Policy to provide and improve advertising services.

Google Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data Sharing

DiceWrite does not sell your personal information. We do not share your personal information with third parties except as required for services such as Google AdMob.

Children's Privacy

Rollsmith is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. Because most Rollsmith data is stored locally on your device, you remain in control of your information.

Your Choices

  • You may disable microphone permission at any time through your device settings.
  • You may remove custom backgrounds from within the app.
  • You may uninstall the application at any time.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
